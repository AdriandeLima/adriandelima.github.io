October 25, 2024

—

Malotte’s The Lord’s Prayer

Composed in 1935 by Albert Hay Malotte, The Lord’s Prayer sets the classic prayer given by Christ in Matthew 6 to song. This rendition is sung by my father, with myself at the piano.

Lyrics:

Our Father,
Which art in heaven,
Hallowed be
Thy name,
Thy kingdom come
Thy will be done,
On earth
As it is
In Heaven.
Give us this day,
Our daily bread.
And forgive us our debts,
As we
Forgive our debtors.
And lead us not
Into temptation.
But deliver us from evil.
For thine
Is the Kingdom,
And the power,
And the glory,
Forever,
Amen.
