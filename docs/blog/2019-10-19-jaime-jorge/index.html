Jaime Jorge concert collaboration (2019)

October 19, 2019

—

Towards the end of 2019, my little brother Adrian played in a concert starring Jaime Jorge, a well-known Adventist musician with a music ministry that takes him around the world. The concert was organised by Pastor James Shepley of Cheltenham Seventh-Day Adventist Church.

I was studying at Newbold College of Higher Education on the Year In Mission program, and decided to drive down to see the concert with my friend and classmate Jan. It was a bit of a long drive from Binfield (near Reading), but we had BBC Radio 3 and For the Ones by Yelfris Vald√©s to keep us company.

The concert was very nice, with my little brother Adrian and friends from the youth of Cheltenham church backing Jaime Jorge for a few pieces, and it was great to see family again for the first time in a few months. I distinctly remember Mum got her car locked behind a gate because parking was a bit funny there.

– Darren, 31 Aug 2025
